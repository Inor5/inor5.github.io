Facts About the City
Population: Baton Rouge has a population of 221,453 people as of 2022
Founded: The foundation of Baton Rouge, Louisiana, dates to 1721. The city got its name after French explorers traveled up the Mississippi River and saw a bloodied, red pole on the shore. The "red stick" was marking the boundaries between hunting grounds of two Native tribes.
Location: Baton Rouge is Located on the eastern bank of the Mississippi River.
Classification: Baton Rouge is an urban area and the capital of Louisiana.
Average income: [Below state average:53k]The average salary in Baton Rouge, LA is $41,651 per year according to 2023 American Community Survey 1-Year Estimates.
