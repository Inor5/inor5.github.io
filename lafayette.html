Facts About the City
Population: Lafayette has a population of 121,389 people as of 2022.
Founded: Lafayette, Louisiana was founded in 1821 as Vermilionville, and renamed Lafayette in 1884.
Location: this city is located along the Vermilion River.
Classification: Lafayette, Louisiana is considered an urban area.
Average income: [Below state average:53k] The average salary in Lafayette, LA is $41,651 per year according to 2023 American Community Survey 1-Year Estimates.
