Facts About the City
Population: New Orleans has a population of 369,749 people as of 2022
Founded: New Orleans was "founded" by the French in 1718 before becoming a part of the U.S. in the Louisiana Purchase of 1803.
Location: New Orleans is located in Southeast Louisiana.
Classification: The majority of Louisiana is made up of rural areas and communities, but the city of New Orleans is known to be an urban area.
Average income: [Above state average:53k]The average salary in New Orleans, LA is $55,580 per year according to 2023 American Community Survey 1-Year Estimates.
